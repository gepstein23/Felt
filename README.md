> Speak a hand. Get a visual replay. Get coached. In seconds.

---

Poker players have been screenshotting hand histories and typing paragraphs into group chats since forever. **Felt ends that.**

Describe any hand by voice — what you held, how it played out, what you were thinking — and Felt turns it into a clean, visual breakdown with AI coaching on every decision. No more walls of text. No more "wait which street was that?"

---

## What You Can Do

**🎙️ Speak your hand**
Just talk. Felt listens, parses, and structures the hand automatically.

**🃏 See it visually**
Cards, positions, actions street by street, pot running in real time. Clean enough to screenshot and share anywhere.

**🧠 Get coached**
AI breaks down every key decision — what you did, what it thinks, what to consider next time.

**📂 Import your history**
Already have hands from PokerStars or GGPoker? Upload in bulk.

**🔗 Share instantly**
One link. Anyone can view the hand — no account needed.
